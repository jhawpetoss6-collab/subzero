Privacy Policy

SubZero is a locally powered AI chat and crypto wallet companion. We designed it to keep your data on your device whenever possible.

Data Collection

  • SubZero does not collect, sell, or share your personal information.
  • Chat history is stored locally on your device. You can clear it any time from Settings.
  • Payment actions (Google Pay, Cash App, Venmo, Netspend, crypto) are processed by their respective providers. SubZero does not see your full card or account numbers.

Permissions

  • NFC: used for optional tap‑to‑pay between devices.
  • Camera: used only to scan QR codes for payments.
  • Storage: used to cache the PWA for offline use.

Third‑Party Services

Payments are handled by third parties you choose (e.g., Google Pay / Stripe, Cash App, Venmo, your crypto wallet). Their terms and privacy policies apply when you use them.
